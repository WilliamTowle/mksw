* Wondermedia WM8650 "android laptop"
- single core ARM CPU (armv5te); 2.6.32 kernel sources published
- kernel config needs CONFIG_ARCH_WM8505 (ARCH_VT8500, CPU_ARM926T)
- devices boot with U-Boot, will autorun custom boot scripts from SSD
- thorough HOWTO at https://github.com/projectgus/kernel_wm8505
